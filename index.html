Pharaoh in Nutshell.

I am a dedicated data analyst passionate about analyzing complex data and storytelling. I believe that words and data are the two most powerful tools to change the world when given the right intent and audience. In my entire career, I have blended my expertise to help organizations interpret data to drive meaningful decisions.

Delivering results. By the end of the day, I work on simplifying complex tasks through programming, analyzing large datasets, and uncovering valuable insights. My expertise spans around data analysis, visualization, machine learning, and problem solving. I specialize in end-to-end data analytics projects—from collecting data from third-party sources to delivering actionable insights, bridging the gap between raw data and decision-making.

Beyond work. After work, I dedicate my time to personal projects, continuous learning, and competitive programming. I challenge myself on platforms like HackerRank and explore new skills to stay ahead in the evolving data landscape. Most of my learning has come from online platforms—e-books, video tutorials, and self-paced courses. I deeply appreciate the democratization of education that has made these resources widely accessible.

Projects completed. Explore some of my data analytics projects on the projects section. I have linked my original work to their GitHub repositories, so feel free to download, explore, and experiment with my code.

Giving back to the community. I believe in sharing knowledge and supporting the data community. I write blogs on data analytics trends, mentor junior analysts on turning complex data into actionable insights, and help teams build dashboards, spot trends, and find data-driven answers. I make data work for people—whether it's through insightful reports, user-friendly dashboards, or answering tough business questions.
