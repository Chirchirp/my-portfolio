About Me

I am a data analyst with a passion for complex data analysis and storytelling. I believe that words and data are the two most powerful tools to change the world when given the right intent and audience. Over the past 7 years, I have helped organizations interpret data to drive meaningful decisions.

By the end of the day, I work on simplifying complex tasks through programming, analyzing large datasets, and uncovering valuable insights. My expertise spans data analysis, visualization, machine learning, and problem solving. I specialize in end-to-end data analytics projects—from collecting data from third-party sources to delivering actionable insights, bridging the gap between raw data and decision-making.
